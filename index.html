02 · THE SCIENCE

Not a fertiliser.
A precision delivery system.

Agrarius™ works at 1 gram per hectare, not because it is a powerful chemical, but because it is not a chemical input at all. The active signalling molecule is encapsulated in a proprietary nano-encapsulation carbohydrate coating. It dissolves only on contact with plant physiology. Nothing is lost before it reaches the plant.

Once inside, the molecule travels bidirectionally through both the xylem and the phloem, reaching every part of the plant from a single foliar spray. The plant does not receive a nutrient. It receives a signal. The biology does the rest.
