THE SCIENCE

What Agrarius™ Actually Does

250+ third-party field trials. 89% success rate. The mechanism is real.

Agrarius™ is a humalite-derived biostimulant, humalite being the unhumified geological precursor to humic acid, suspended in water. It does not replace fertiliser. It does not feed the plant directly. It changes the information environment the plant operates in.

When applied as a foliar spray, Agrarius™ enters through leaf stomata and is transported through both xylem (upward, water-mineral flow) and phloem (downward, sugar-metabolite flow) simultaneously, a dual vascular pathway confirmed in the March 2026 MVMD technical review. This bidirectional transport means the signal reaches the root zone within hours, not days.

At the root zone, the compound propagates through existing mycorrhizal networks. The plant receives a stress-priming signal. It upregulates heat shock proteins and antioxidant enzymes. It enters a state of heightened biological readiness.

The plant does not work harder. It works smarter.
